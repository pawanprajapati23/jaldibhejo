## AdSense Approval Checklist

- Publish Privacy Policy, Terms & Conditions, About Us, Contact Us, and Disclaimer.
- Keep navigation clear and accessible from every page.
- Add sitemap.xml and robots.txt.
- Publish original content on tool pages, file sharing page, and blog posts.
- Avoid copied or thin content.
- Do not place ads too close to buttons such as Send, Receive, Download, Copy, or Generate.
- Do not ask users to click ads.
- Mention cookies, Google AdSense, and Analytics in the Privacy Policy.
- Keep the site fast, mobile-friendly, and usable before applying.

## Ad Placement Strategy

Use ads only after AdSense approval and after adding your publisher ID.

Recommended placements:

- Blog pages: after the introduction, between long sections, and near the end.
- Tool pages: below the tool interface and after SEO content sections.
- Category pages: after the first row of tool cards and near the footer.
- File Sharing page: below informational content, never beside the Send/Receive action buttons.

Avoid:

- Ads directly above or below download buttons.
- Sticky ads that cover content.
- Layouts that cause accidental clicks.
